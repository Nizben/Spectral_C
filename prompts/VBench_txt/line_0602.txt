A time-lapse video of The Bund in Shanghai, starting with a wide shot of the iconic waterfront area at dusk. The scene gradually shifts focus from the bustling crowd and buildings in the foreground to the serene Huangpu River and the illuminated skyline of Pudong in the background. The camera maintains a static position, emphasizing the dynamic flow of people and vehicles as well as the reflective surfaces of the water. The transition between different focal points highlights the changing atmosphere and lights of the city.
