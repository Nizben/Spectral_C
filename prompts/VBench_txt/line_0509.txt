A watercolor painting of The Bund in Shanghai, showcasing the iconic skyline with historic buildings and modern skyscrapers reflected in the calm waters of the Huangpu River. The scene includes the clock tower of the Peace Hotel and other architectural landmarks, with soft pastel hues capturing the serene atmosphere during a golden hour. The waterfront promenade is lightly populated with locals walking and tourists taking photos. The background depicts a mix of traditional shikumen houses and bustling streets. Medium shot, static scene.
