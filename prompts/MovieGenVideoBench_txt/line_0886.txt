A serene watercolor painting depicting a mother otter floating gracefully on her back in a tranquil river. The otter cradles her playful pup on her stomach, gently keeping it warm and safe in the gentle current. The pup's small paws dangle in the water, while the mother's fur glistens in the soft sunlight. The background features a lush forest with tall trees reflected in the river, and a few wildflowers dotting the banks. The water has a soft, ethereal quality, emphasizing the peacefulness of the scene. A medium shot capturing the tender interaction between the mother and her pup from a slightly overhead angle.
