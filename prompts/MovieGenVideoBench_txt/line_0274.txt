A vibrant and dynamic digital art piece depicting sea creatures made of crystal swimming gracefully in an ocean. The crystal animals include a schools of shimmering fish, a graceful mermaid, and a majestic sea dragon, all moving fluidly in the water. The mermaid has long, flowing hair and scales that sparkle like diamonds. The sea dragon has a sleek, serpentine body with wings that glisten under the sunlight. The ocean is clear and deep, with hints of coral and seaweed in the background, creating a magical underwater world. The camera angle is from a low, sweeping perspective, capturing the movement and beauty of the crystal beings as they swim together.
