A surreal digital artwork in a vibrant, thick painting style depicting a large, humanoid green monster composed of intertwining plant life, walking through a bustling airport. The monster has multiple arms and legs, each ending in grasping vines, with leaves and flowers adorning its body. It moves with a determined gait, its eyes glowing with a soft, ethereal light. The airport background is filled with passengers, luggage carts, and the hum of activity, with blurred reflections in the glass windows. The lighting is warm and inviting, contrasting with the eerie nature of the monster. A dynamic medium shot with the monster seen from a slightly elevated angle, capturing its natural, fluid movements.
